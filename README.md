#EcoClean Solutions

### Sustainable Cleaning for a Better Tomorrow  

EcoClean Solutions is an innovative project dedicated to promoting eco-friendly cleaning practices through technology. The project aims to reduce the environmental impact of cleaning products and services by providing data-driven insights, sustainable alternatives, and automation tools that support a greener planet.  

---

##Features  

- **Eco-Friendly Product Recommendations:** Suggests sustainable cleaning products and alternatives.  
- **Smart Waste Management:** Tracks and optimizes resource usage to minimize waste.  
- **Automation Tools:** Automates cleaning tasks for efficiency and environmental safety.  
- **Data Analytics Dashboard:** Visualizes cleaning performance, carbon savings, and usage trends.  
- **Sustainability Reports:** Generates reports to measure and showcase eco-impact.
